Climate Change Awareness

Protect Our Planet – Act Now!

What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. While natural causes contribute, human activities like burning fossil fuels have significantly accelerated the process.

Why It Matters

Rising global temperatures are causing glaciers to melt, sea levels to rise, and extreme weather events to become more frequent. Climate change threatens ecosystems, health, and the global economy.

What You Can Do

  • Reduce energy consumption
  • Use Electric Vehicles
  • Use public transport or cycle
  • Minimize plastic use
  • Support renewable energy initiatives
  • Spread awareness in your community

Global Actions

International efforts like the Paris Agreement aim to reduce carbon emissions and keep global warming below 1.5°C. It's vital for nations to work together for a sustainable future.

Join the Movement

Be part of the change! Join environmental organizations, participate in clean-up drives, or share your voice online to influence others to act.
